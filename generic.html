Doctor Chat Bot

Your Personal Chat Bot

To access the chat bot, please wait for the Messanger logo to appear on the bottom right of the screen. Once it appears, click on it and sign in or create a Messanger account to use with the chat bot.

When conversing with the chat bot, you will have 3 different options to choose from:

Scheduling an appointment: You may schedule an apointment with a patient, by proving the appointment data and patient name

ICU Risk calculator: Enter details of patient to have our advanced machine learning model predict the need for ICU of patient

Symptom Checker: Chat with a GPT powered AI to get quick answers to any medical related queries
